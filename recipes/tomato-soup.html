Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz (about 800g) canned whole tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the canned tomatoes, breaking them up with a spoon. Add vegetable broth, sugar, dried basil, and dried oregano. Stir well.
  4. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender in batches, blend, and return to the pot.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and heat the soup gently without boiling.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
