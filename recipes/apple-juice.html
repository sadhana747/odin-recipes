Apple Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 medium-sized apples (choose a sweet variety)
  • Cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: Mint leaves for garnish

    • Instructions:

    1. Prepare the Apples:
      • Wash the apples thoroughly under running water.
      • If desired, peel the apples for a smoother texture, or you can leave the peel on for added nutrients and color.
      • Core the apples and remove the seeds.
      • Cut the apples into bite-sized pieces. The size can vary based on your preference, but aim for small chunks that are easy to blend.
    2. Chill the Apple Pieces:
      • Place the bite-sized apple pieces in a bowl and refrigerate them for at least 30 minutes. Chilled apples will make the juice more refreshing.
    3. Blend the Apple Juice:
      • Once the apple pieces are chilled, transfer them to a blender.
      • Add a splash of cold water to help with blending.
      • Blend on high speed until the apples are completely pureed and the juice is smooth.
    4. Strain the Juice (Optional):
      • If you prefer a smoother texture, strain the apple puree through a fine mesh strainer to remove any larger pieces or pulp. This step is optional and depends on your preference.
    5. Serve the Cold Apple Juice:
      • Fill glasses with ice cubes for an extra chilled experience.
      • Pour the freshly blended or strained apple juice into the glasses.
    6. Garnish and Enjoy:
      • If desired, garnish the apple juice with a few mint leaves for a burst of freshness and aroma.
      • Stir the juice gently before drinking to ensure an even distribution of flavors.
    7. Serve Immediately:
      • Apple juice is best enjoyed immediately while it's cold and at its freshest.
      • Sip and savor the deliciousness of your homemade cold and tasty apple juice with delightful bite-sized apple pieces!
