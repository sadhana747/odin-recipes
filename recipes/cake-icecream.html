Ice-Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

Cake Base Ingredients:
  • All-purpose flour - 1 cup
  • Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Unsalted butter, softened - 1/2 cup
  • Granulated sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Large eggs - 2
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  • Milk - 1/4 cup
Ice Cream Base Ingredients:
  • Heavy cream - 2 cups
  • Sweetened condensed milk - 1 can (14 oz)
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
Fresh Fruits:
  • Strawberries, chopped
  • Blueberries
  • Kiwi, diced
  • Mango, diced
  • Pineapple, diced

Instructions:

Prepare the Cake Base:
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the following dry ingredients:
    • All-purpose flour - 1 cup
    • Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
    • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
  3. In a separate larger bowl, cream together the following:
    • Unsalted butter, softened - 1/2 cup
    • Granulated sugar - 1/2 cup
  4. Beat in the following wet ingredients one by one:
    • Large eggs - 2
    • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  5. Gradually add the dry flour mixture to the wet ingredients, alternating with:
    • Milk - 1/4 cup
    Mix until just combined.
  6. Pour the cake batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for about 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool completely, then cut it into small cubes. Set aside.
Prepare the Ice Cream Base:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, whip the following until stiff peaks form:
    • Heavy cream - 2 cups
  2. Gently fold in the following until well combined:
    • Sweetened condensed milk - 1 can (14 oz)
    • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
Assemble the Fresh Fruit Cake Ice Cream:
  1. In a freezer-safe container, layer the following:
    • Cake cubes
    • Mixed fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, mango, pineapple)
    • Ice cream mixture
    Repeat the layers until you use up all the ingredients.
  2. Use a spatula to gently swirl the layers together, creating a marbled effect.
  3. Cover the container with plastic wrap or a lid and freeze the ice cream cake for at least 6 hours or until firm.
Serve:
  1. Prior to serving, let the ice cream cake sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly.
  2. Scoop the Fresh Fruit Cake Ice Cream into bowls or onto dessert plates.
  3. Garnish with additional fresh fruit if desired.
  4. Enjoy your homemade Fresh Fruit Cake Ice Cream!
