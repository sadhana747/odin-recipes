Chili Garlic Honey Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Description

Chili garlic honey roasted potatoes are crispy potato chunks tossed in a delightful blend of spicy chili, fragrant garlic, and sweet honey, resulting in a flavorful and irresistible side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs (about 700g) baby potatoes, washed and halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, chili powder, honey, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
  3. Add the halved baby potatoes to the bowl and toss them in the marinade until they are evenly coated.
  4. Place the coated potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Roast the potatoes in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. Make sure to stir the potatoes halfway through for even cooking.
  6. Once the potatoes are cooked, remove them from the oven and transfer them to a serving dish.
  7. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for added freshness and color.
  8. Serve the chili garlic honey roasted potatoes as a delightful side dish.
